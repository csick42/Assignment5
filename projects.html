FinTime – AI-powered Financial Advisor (Honors I‑Core)

Kelley’s I‑Core integrates Finance, Marketing, Operations, and Leadership in a rigorous, semester‑long program. In Fall 2023 Honors I‑Core, my team acted as entrepreneurs and created an AI‑powered financial advisor app called FinTime to deliver personalized guidance.

The semester culminated in a final pitch to a panel of industry leaders and AI specialists. Our work was featured by the Kelley Indianapolis blog. See the write‑up: Kelley Indianapolis Honors students pitch AI product development projects to experts.

Key Deliverables

  • Final Capital Budgeting (.xlsx) — financial investment, projected cash flows (5 years), and sensitivity to required return, unit sales, fixed/variable costs, and price.
  • FinTime Executive Brief (.docx) — value proposition; AI‑driven efficiency; planning tools; real‑time monitoring; client‑centric design.
  • Final Presentation (.pptx) — features, timeline, marketing strategy, financial projections, competitive positioning, and recommendation.

Source and citation: Kelley Indianapolis blog linked above.

The Effects of Social Media on the Health of College Students

This analysis explores relationships between social media use and mental well‑being among college students. The dataset originates from a research study hosted on OpenSIUC (a publicly accessible academic repository).
