About Me

I have always had a deep appreciation for trees. The resilience they display, bending and swaying gracefully but never breaking in the face of storms, mirrors how I handle challenges that come my way. On projects, I enjoy the camaraderie formed as well as the opportunities to grow and learn inherent when challenges arise.

As an IU graduate student, I am pursuing an MSIS (Master of Science in Information Systems) from the Kelley School of Business. My passion lies in the power of storytelling, where I aim to impact people by leveraging technology to solve business problems.

I am a storyteller in the business world who can manage diverse deadlines across multiple groups and consistently deliver exceptional results.
